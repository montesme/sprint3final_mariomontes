Snowy Cream Shop

Snowy Cream Sho was born with the purpose that people change the concept of ice cream and lose their fear of it so that they have sweeter moments in their lives. For this purpose we are dedicated to making the best ice cream possible, which is why we see ice cream as a main dish and not as a dessert. We want to become the favorite ice cream parlor for ice cream lovers, recalling a little the concept of coffee shops and Italian gelaterias where people meet to chat and share unique moments, with a differential of innovation and service.
